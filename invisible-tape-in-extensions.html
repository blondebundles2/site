How to Apply Invisible Tape in Extensions?

Invisible tape in hair extensions is a popular and versatile method for attaching hair extensions. The tape is applied directly to the scalp and the hair extension is then glued to the tape. This method is quick and easy and can be done at home.

To apply invisible tape in extensions, start by cleanse your scalp with a clarifying shampoo. This will remove any oils or product build-up that could prevent the tape from adhering properly. Next, measure out the length of tape you need and cut it into strips. Apply the strips of tape to your scalp, starting at the back of your head and working forward. Be sure to smooth down the edges of the tape so that they are flush against your skin.

Now it's time to apply the hair extensions. Start by taking a small section of hair and apply a generous amount of glue to the weft. Press the weft onto the strip of tape and hold for 30 seconds to allow the glue to set. Repeat this process until all of your hair extensions are in place.
